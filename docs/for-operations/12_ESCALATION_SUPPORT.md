## 12. Escalation and Support

### 12.1 IT Support Tiers

| Tier | Responsibility | Availability | Escalation |
|------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| **L1: Helpdesk** | User questions, password resets | 8 AM - 5 PM Mon-Fri | L2 if technical issue |
| **L2: IT Operations** | Infrastructure monitoring, maintenance | 24/7 on-call rotation | L3 if complex |
| **L3: Engineering** | Code changes, architecture decisions | On-demand | L4 if critical |
| **L4: IT Director** | Budget, vendor negotiations, strategic | Business hours | Executive team |

### 12.2 External Vendor Escalation

- **Google Cloud:** Premium Support (24/7).
- **Anthropic:** Enterprise (email only).
- **Microsoft (Azure AD):** Premier Support.
